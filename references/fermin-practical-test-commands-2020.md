## Source

- **Document:** *Practical Driving Test Student Guide*
- **Publisher:** Autoescuela Fermín
- **Date shown:** 2020 (PDF created 3 March 2020)
- **Local source supplied by Jeffrey:** `20200303 Fermin - Practical Driving Test Student Guide.pdf`
- **Scope:** Eight-page bilingual student guide covering test structure,
  prechecks, common driving instructions, terminology, and vocabulary.

The PDF is not stored in this repository. This note preserves the relevant
Spanish text and its relationship to the game's provisional `COMMANDS` set.

## Source limitations

The guide labels the phrases as examples, common instructions, and terminology.
It is useful evidence from Jeffrey's autoescuela, but it does not claim to be an
exhaustive or verbatim transcript of every instruction a DGT examiner in Oviedo
may use. Backlog item 1 should not be considered instructor-verified solely on
the basis of this document.

## Verbatim extraction

Capitalization and wording below follow the PDF. Parenthetical alternatives
have been expanded only in the comparison table.

### Exterior precheck examples (PDF page 3)

- `Localizar: Batería, Liquido de frenos, Nivel de aceite y Nivel de líquido refrigerante`

### Interior precheck examples (PDF page 4)

- `Abrir el capo y decir qué niveles se deben revisar`
- `Dónde comprobar nivel combustible o temperatura motor`
- `Abrir el maletero`
- `Bloqueo o desbloqueo infantil de elevalunas traseros`
- `Encender cualquier luz: largo alcance, niebla delantera o trasera`
- `Cómo desempañar la luna delantera o trasera`

### Common exam instructions (PDF page 5)

- `Tome la primera (segunda, tercera) salida en la glorieta`
- `Gire a la derecha (o izquierda) cuando pueda`
- `Realice una parada (o estacionamiento)`
- `Inmovilice el vehículo`
- `Finalización del examen`

The explanatory text under `Inmovilice el vehículo` says to apply the
handbrake, switch off the engine, and leave the gear lever in gear or position
P. `Finalización del examen` is presented as an instruction heading, although
it reads as a label rather than a complete spoken command.

The same page also gives these rules for interpreting examiner guidance:

- `El examinador guiará el recorrido`
- `Los primeros 10 minutos pueden ser libres`
- `En caso de la existencia de señales de indicación nos indicará por ellas`
- `En caso de no decir nada deberemos continuar de frente`

### Indications terminology (PDF page 6)

- `Primera (segunda, tercera, cuarta, quinta) salida`
- `Cambio de sentido`
- `Parada`
- `Estacionamiento`
- `Detención`
- `Adelantamiento`
- `Adapte la velocidad a la vía`
- `Volante recto`

The guide distinguishes `Parada` (a voluntary stop, with up to two minutes to
manoeuvre and without reversing) from `Detención` (an involuntary stop). It
describes `Estacionamiento` as parking with reversing.

## Guide material absent from the starter set

- Fifth-exit terminology
- `Inmovilice el vehículo`
- `Volante recto`
- Exterior component-location check
- Opening the bonnet and identifying fluid levels
- Locating the fuel-level and engine-temperature displays
- Opening the boot
- Operating the rear-window child lock
- Selecting high-beam or front/rear fog lights
- Demisting the front or rear window

These should be evaluated separately from the driving-direction cards because
the guide presents most of them as precheck questions or demonstrations.
